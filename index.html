About

ICITRI 2022 is an International Conference for sharing knowledges and researches in Computer and Information Science and provide a platform for researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and shares the cutting-edge development of Computer and Information Science research.

SUBMISSION

Accepted paper will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore subject to meeting IEEE Xplores scope and quality requirements

AUTHOR GUIDELINE

Please prepare your manuscripts according to the IEEE Conference Paper format. The manuscript should be 4 to 6 pages long. Research paper should explore a specific technology problem and propose a complete solution to it, with experimental results.

Download Templates

ICITRI POLICY

Your submission will be blind-reviewed by at least three independent reviewers. The decision on your paper will be informed via email by the dates listed in the Home section.

NO-SHOW POLICY

The accepted papers that are not presented in the conference will not be included in the final Proceeding and it will not be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore.
